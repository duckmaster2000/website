Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 29, 2026

What Data We Collect

  • Account details you enter at registration: email, username, full name, birthday, and hashed password.
  • Session data needed to keep you logged in while browsing.
  • Track profile selections (name/gender selection used for dashboard personalization).
  • Local preferences such as achievements and citations lists if you use editable sections.

Why We Collect It

  • Authenticate users and protect access to personalized pages.
  • Remember consent and user choices across visits.
  • Provide track dashboard personalization and saved settings.

How Data Is Stored

  • This site currently stores data in your browser (cookies and local storage).
  • Passwords are stored as salted hashes in local storage, not plaintext.
  • No third-party analytics or advertising cookies are enabled by default.

Your Choices

  • You can decline cookies; some site features will not be available.
  • You can clear cookies and local storage anytime in browser settings.
  • You can log out at any time using the logout control shown on authenticated pages.
